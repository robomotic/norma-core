Build target:
  bitbake x8-normacore

Before building, create local access files in the build directory:

  conf/local-rootpw.inc
    X8_ROOT_HASH = "\$6\$..."

  conf/root-authorized_keys
    ssh-ed25519 ...

  conf/local-secrets.inc
    X8_ROOT_AUTHORIZED_KEYS_FILE = "${TOPDIR}/conf/root-authorized_keys"

The image build intentionally fails if either X8_ROOT_HASH or
X8_ROOT_AUTHORIZED_KEYS_FILE is missing or empty.

Flash prep after a successful build:
  cp -Lf tmp/deploy/images/portenta-x8/x8-normacore-portenta-x8.rootfs.wic.zst /path/to/flash-x8/x8-clean-hw-image-portenta-x8.wic.zst
  zstd -d -f /path/to/flash-x8/x8-clean-hw-image-portenta-x8.wic.zst -o /path/to/flash-x8/x8-clean-hw-image-portenta-x8.wic
  cp -f tmp/deploy/images/portenta-x8/imx-boot-portenta-x8-sd.bin-flash_evk /path/to/flash-x8/imx-boot-x8-clean.bin
